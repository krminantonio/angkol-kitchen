MERYENDA MEALS

PASTA

CARBONARA

This pasta dish consists of curated pork mushroom and cream sauce that adds a silky texture to the pasta. It also comes with 2 free garlic bread that perfectly pairs the dish and will surely leave you satisfied.

SPAGHETTI

This pasta is composed of a Filipino Style Spaghetti that comes with meatballs made out of a combination of ground meat ,bread crumbs, egg and other seasonings topped with cheese and parsley for garnish. It also comes with 2 free garlic bread that brings out an additional flavor to the meal.

PESTO

This pasta consists of sauteed chicken cut into bite sized that is seasoned with salt and pepper coated with basil pesto sauce and topped with garlic parmesan that makes it creamier and cheesier and parsley that adds an extra flavor.

BURGERS AND FRIES

FRENCH FRIES

Angkol’s french fries are topped with ketchup and mayo, deeply fried, crispy and salted for added flavor. French Fries can be paired with anything and be eaten at any time. CRISPY CHICKEN BURGER

Angkol’s Crispy Chicken Burger is generously filled with mayo sauce, fresh vegetables , a large soft bun and chicken fillet that is crispy and bursting with flavour. This dish is perfect for a snack in the middle of the day and as usual with Angkol’s meal you get more money’s worth.
