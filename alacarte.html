ALA CARTE

RICE MEALS

CORDON BLEU

Angkol’ Cordon Bleu is a perfectly balanced out chicken roll that is swirling with melted cheese. It is a dish of meat wrapped around cheese, then breaded and pan-fried or deep-fried,wrapped around a slice of ham and a slice of cheese, breaded, and then pan fried.

Burger Steak

Angkol’s Burger Steak is composed of a combination of ground pork and ground beef that produces a savory flavor. It is also served with a vegetables on the side, a cup of warm rice, garnished with parsley and most importantly topped with Angkol’s flavourful gravy with mushroom that perfectly pairs the peppery flavor of the burger patty

Sisig

Angkol’s Pork Sisig is a Filipino dish composed of mainly of pig’s parts such as minced pork meat, ears, and face. Chicken liver, onions, and chili peppers are also added. It is served with fried egg, a dash of mayo, a cup of warm rice and vegetables on the side.

FRIED CHICKEN

Angkol’s Fried Chicken is spiced perfectly that makes this fried chicken one of a kind. It is also topped with their flavourful sauce that perfectly matches the juicyness of their checken. This dish is served with warm rice, garnished with parsely and served with a side of vegetables.

Fish Fillet

Angkol’s Fish Fillet is a simple and healthy meal that is perfect for people who are on a diet and want to stay out of fatty meats. This dish is served with a cup of warm rice and vegetables on the side to add a dash of freshness to the meal.

BESTSELLING MEALS

CHICKEN LOLLIPOP

Angkol’s Chicken lollipops are crispy mini fried chicken perfect for an ala carte snack or an easy go to snack for the day. This chicken lollipop is covered with crispy breadcrumbs and spiced that will surely bring out a savoury flavor for each bite.

CHICKEN WINGS

Try out Angkol’s chicken wings that are generously spiced and sauced. It consists of 6pcs. wings and Available Flavors are Honey Glazed (sweet), BBQ (Sweet & tangy), Buffalo (Sweet & Spicy) and Garlic Parmesan

CHICKEN KARAAGE

Try out Angkol’s chicken wings that are generously spiced and sauced. It consists of 6pcs. wings and Available Flavors are Honey Glazed (sweet), BBQ (Sweet & tangy), Buffalo (Sweet & Spicy) and Garlic Parmesan.

TONKATSU

Angkol’s Tonkatsu is a Japanese style pork cutlet dish that has been coated in flaky panko breadcrumbs then deep-fried. It is served with warm rice, vegetables and a dash of ketsup to perfectly pair the savoury pork flavour.

About Us

Angkol's Kitchen started on early September 2019 during a pandemic. It was a big risk to take especially for starting business owners.Nonetheless, owners Karen Caballes and Jerome Albovias took the chance to establish a food business which eventually started to take off and gain popularity among their neighborhood.
